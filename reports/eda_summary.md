# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Summary

## Project

Mutual Fund Analytics Platform

## Objective

The objective of this exploratory data analysis is to understand the structure, quality, and distribution of the mutual fund datasets before performing advanced analytics and dashboard development.

---

# Datasets Used

* fund_master.csv
* nav_history.csv
* fund_returns.csv
* aum_data.csv
* expense_ratios.csv
* investor_transactions.csv
* benchmark_returns.csv
* risk_metrics.csv
* scheme_categories.csv
* fund_manager_details.csv

---

# Summary Statistics

Descriptive statistics were generated for the numerical datasets including:

* Assets Under Management (AUM)
* NAV values
* Expense Ratios
* Fund Returns

The analysis included:

* Count
* Mean
* Standard Deviation
* Minimum
* Maximum
* Quartiles

# Key Findings

* Equity funds dominate the available schemes.
* Most schemes belong to High Risk category.
* NAV values vary significantly across schemes.
* AUM distribution indicates concentration among major fund houses.
* Expense ratios remain within expected regulatory limits.
* Monthly SIP investments show an increasing trend.
* Investor participation varies across different states.
* Diversification across sectors is clearly visible.

---

# Conclusion

The datasets were successfully explored and visualized using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn and Plotly. The generated insights will be used for the Fund Performance Analytics and Dashboard development phases.
